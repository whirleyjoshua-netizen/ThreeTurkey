Frequently Asked Questions

How does Marqet access my data?

You sign in with Google, which grants us read-only access to your Analytics and Search Console data. We never modify anything. You can revoke access from your Google account at any time.

Do I need to be an SEO expert?

Not at all. Marqet translates complex SEO data into plain-English recommendations anyone can follow. Each fix includes what to change, why it matters, and the expected impact.

How is this different from SEMrush or Ahrefs?

Those tools give you raw data and expect you to know what to do with it. Marqet reads your actual traffic data and tells you exactly what to fix, ranked by impact. Think of it as an SEO consultant that never sleeps — at 1/50th the cost.
